A Guide to the Constellations

Click on the constellation name

Constellations are a very interesting part of Astronomy that actually provides many of the roots for the other "Astro" science! Of course by other "Astro" science, I mean Astrology! Take time to study the Constellations map seen above and remember to click on each constellation or planet and be transported instantly to the Wikipedia page that will explain much more about each of the items that are shown on this really useful map! The stars are more than just balls of combustable gasses and radiation, they were, and still are, some of the most important and central objects for many civilizations, past, present, and most certainly the future that we have found in the Universe! Did you know that most of the stars that you see in the night sky have already died? I know that sounds confusing since we still see them twinkling and burning at night! Bear in mind how far away the majority of those stars are from us on Earth! The time it takes for the light from the final days of these star to reach us on Earth is so long that when we finally do see it here on Earth, the star has probably been dead for quite some time!!! Pretty cool, huh?
